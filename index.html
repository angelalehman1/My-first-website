Behind the Business

At Layman's Terms Bookkeeping Services, I bring over 20 years of experience as an Accounting & Administration Manager and Financial Controller, working across a wide range of industries and business environments. My background includes working with construction-related companies, contractors, medical providers, non-profits, and service-based businesses, with experience spanning multiple U.S. states as well as international operations.

Throughout my career, I have worked closely with small business owners and CEOs, providing reliable financial insight and hands-on support. I specialize in producing clear financial reports, cash flow analysis, and customized reporting tailored to each business’s unique needs—so decision-making is based on accurate, understandable information.

Whether expanding or streamlining, I guide businesses in reshaping accounting and administrative roles and designing efficient processes that drive alignment with their size, goals, and operational priorities.

My goal is to simplify financial processes, create clarity, and give business owners the confidence and time to focus on running and growing their business.
